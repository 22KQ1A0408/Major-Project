About Us

Welcome to KFC

At KFC, we're passionate about making great-tasting chicken. Since 1952, we've been serving up our famous Original Recipe chicken, hand-breaded and freshly prepared in our kitchens every day.

But KFC is more than just delicious chicken. We're a global brand with a rich history and a commitment to serving our customers with excellence.

Our History

Founded by Colonel Harland Sanders, KFC began as a humble roadside restaurant in Corbin, Kentucky. Colonel Sanders' secret blend of 11 herbs and spices quickly gained popularity, leading to the expansion of the KFC brand.

Today, KFC is one of the world's largest fast-food chains, with thousands of restaurants in over 140 countries.

Our Mission

Our mission at KFC is to provide our customers with delicious, freshly prepared food served in a welcoming environment. We strive to make every dining experience memorable, whether you're enjoying a bucket of chicken with family or grabbing a quick meal on the go.
